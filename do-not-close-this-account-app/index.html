Do Not Close This Account

Last updated: 13 July 2026 — applies to the Android app published by marcorei (Markus Riegel).

Do Not Close This Account is a simple Android app published by marcorei. It shows a short, fixed on-screen message and nothing else. This page explains what the app does with your information.

In short: the app does not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal data. It has no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no network features.

1. What the app does

When you open the app, it displays a fixed reminder message on screen. That is the full functionality. The app does not require setup, does not connect to external services, and does not process user input beyond showing that message.

2. Information we collect

We do not collect personal data. The app does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any other personally identifiable information.

The app also does not:

  • use analytics or crash-reporting SDKs;
  • show advertisements;
  • create user accounts or profiles;
  • read or write files on your device for app-specific purposes;
  • store data locally or include anything in Android backup;
  • send data over the internet.

Because no personal data is collected by the app, there is nothing for us to access, correct, export, or delete on request.

3. Device permissions

The app is designed to work without requesting sensitive Android permissions. It does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, calendar, storage, or location. It also does not store app-specific data on your device, and Android backup is disabled for this app. If a future version ever requested a permission or stored data, this policy would be updated before that version was published.

4. Data processed by Google

The app is distributed through Google Play. Google may process certain technical information when you download, install, or update the app — for example device identifiers, IP addresses, or purchase and delivery metadata. That processing is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy, not by this app.

We do not receive that information from Google in connection with this app.

5. Children

The app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children under the age required by applicable law in their country.

6. Changes to this policy

If the app’s behaviour changes in a way that affects privacy — for example if analytics, networking, or additional permissions were added — this page will be updated and the “Last updated” date at the top will change. Material changes may also be noted in the Play Store listing.

7. Contact

The app is published by marcorei (Markus Riegel). For privacy questions or any other enquiry, contact hey@marcorei.com. Legal and business details are available in the imprint.
